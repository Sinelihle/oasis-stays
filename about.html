About OASIS STAYS

OASIS STAYS helps you take care of your Airbnb property and guests in return for a percentage of your rental income. We take the pressure off you and assist in making sure your Airbnb runs smoothly.

If you are new to Airbnb and don’t have family or friends who can help you out, why not contact us? Whether you want a hand with arranging regular cleaners to attend your property, or with answering guest emails – Our team strives to create a seamless, profitable co-hosting experience while prioritizing guest satisfaction and maintaining excellent property management, meeting all your Airbnb needs.

Responsibilities Of Main Host

Property Offering and Setting Expectations:The host is responsible for offering a well-maintained and furnished property, setting clear expectations for guests, and ensuring safety and regulatory compliance.

Furnishing the Property and Providing Amenities:The host takes responsibility for furnishing the property with essential furniture and amenities, ensuring guests enjoy a comfortable and convenient stay. They provide a range of amenities, including Wi-Fi access, clean towels, linens, toiletries, and a well-equipped kitchen, enhancing the overall guest experience.

Financial Management and Utility Coverage:As a host, responsibilities include managing the property's finances, collecting rental fees, and covering utility costs for a hassle-free guest experience.

Compliance and Regulations:The main host diligently upholds local laws and regulations, staying updated on any changes and fulfilling all legal obligations associated with hosting. They prioritize the maintenance and upkeep of the property, promptly addressing any repair or maintenance needs to ensure a safe and comfortable environment for guests throughout their stay.
