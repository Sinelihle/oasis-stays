Co-Hosting Service

Would you like to rent out your home using Airbnb but do not have the time or the know how?

We handle guest communication, property maintenance, and more, allowing you to maximize your rental income without the stress.

We provide co-hosting services for your holiday rental property on Airbnb creating a bespoke service as individual as your property.

Think a bottle of local wine, chocolates and a personalised welcome card to get your guests talking... and leaving great reviews.

How can we help you?

Enhance Property Management

If you’re someone who is not computer savvy and would prefer to spend less time sitting in front of a screen managing your listing – We enhance property management by handling guest communication, property maintenance, and ensuring a pleasant experience for guests. Our involvement streamlines operations, minimizes issues, and upholds exceptional service standards.

Expand Reach and Visibility

Co-hosts can contribute to marketing and promoting the property to a broader audience. Through our expertise in listing optimization, social media promotion, and online advertising, co-hosts aim to increase the property's visibility, attract more potential guests, and boost bookings.

Provide Expertise and Support

Some hosts work full-time jobs and do not have the flexibility to meet guests at various hours of the day to hand over the keys and welcome them to a property. We can handle the meeting/greeting of guests.Co-hosts bring valuable skills and expertise in areas like property management, hospitality, marketing, and local insights. We aim to elevate the guest experience, maximize rental performance, and provide crucial support to the main host.

Generate Additional Income

We often enter into co-hosting partnerships with the goal of earning supplemental income. By leveraging their skills and resources, we contribute to maximizing the property's rental potential and increasing occupancy rates, thereby generating more revenue for both the co-host and the main host
